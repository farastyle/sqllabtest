Compact status pill for lab identity labels, exercise status, and product visibility indicators.

```jsx
<Badge variant="sqli">SQL Injection</Badge>
<Badge variant="xss">XSS</Badge>
<Badge variant="idor">IDOR</Badge>
<Badge variant="success">✅ Visível</Badge>
<Badge variant="warning">🔒 OCULTO</Badge>
<Badge variant="danger">⚠️ Confidencial</Badge>
<Badge variant="info">Teste 3</Badge>
<Badge variant="neutral">—</Badge>
```

Notable variants:
- `sqli` / `xss` / `idor`: solid lab identity colors (purple gradient, green, orange) with white text
- `success` / `warning` / `danger` / `info`: semantic, light-surface with dark text
- `neutral`: muted gray, default
